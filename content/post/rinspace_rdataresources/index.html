R data resources

With the R community as vibrant and dynamic as it is, there are an increasingly high number of online data resources readily available to users through simple lines of command. The best hub of R packages making online data available to R users is actively developed by the rOpenSci group. This non-profit organization aims at foster[ing] a culture that values open and reproducible research using shared data and reusable software.

In this post, we will explore some valuable geospatial resources that are accessible using R. We do not mean to provide a thorough review of each resource, but rather provide a list and a glimpse into valuable resources for geospatial analyses and providing maps in R. While this list may not be exhaustive, it certainly represents the tools that we use most through our work.
